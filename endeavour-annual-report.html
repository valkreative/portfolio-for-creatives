ENDEAVOUR ANNUAL REPORT

Endeavour is a Kazakhstan-based apparel company that works closely with corporate clients, making anything from school and work uniforms to athletic clothes to winter wear.
The mission was to create a report that would not only act as a financial guide, but also provide visual and tactile interest. With many movable and interactive pieces, the goal is to give any potential future clients and investors an idea of the high quality materials Endeavour uses and produces.

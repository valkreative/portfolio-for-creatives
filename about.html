ABOUT VALENTINA

Hello, I’m Valentina, a Vancouver-based, Russian-Canadian creative.
My mini-manifesto: “I do what I love and love what I do. Existence is a never-ending pool of opportunity for discovery and adventure. Solutions are found in unexpected places; harmony and balance can be teased out of a world of chaos.”
I pride myself on being versatile and resilient. A soon-to-be graduate of the IDEA School of Design at Capilano University, I delight in creating vibrant packaging designs and developing design systems that carry across the brand.
When I’m not busy taking pictures of my cat or fruitlessly trying to keep my hair out of my face, you can find me buried in my sketchbook, coming up with new font designs, or capturing the moment by drawing strangers in public.
If you have a project that needs a designer’s eye and an illustrator’s wrist, let’s grab a coffee and chat!

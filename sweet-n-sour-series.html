STUDIO BREWING SWEET N SOUR SERIES

Studio Brewing focuses on creating accessible, unpretentious craft beers.

As part of its summer collection, Studio launched a series of four different beers. The challenge was to come up with a general look and feel for the series that set it apart from their other beers. The key was to highlight the fun-loving nature of the brewery. Playful, abstract shapes and bright colours created a system of illustrative elements that would work together as a single unit to suggest the fruity summer tanginess of each flavour

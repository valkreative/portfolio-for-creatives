BEGINNER’S GUIDE COCKTAIL RECIPE BOOK

Turning the Fuck up is GenZ speak for partying hard. Beginner’s Guide to Turning the Fuck Up is a pocket-sized manual on how to mix the most popular drinks for almost any function. The goal was to cater to everyone’s taste preferences by categorizing every cocktail by its flavour profile–easily accessible by tabs for sweet, savoury, sour, umami and bitter concoctions.
